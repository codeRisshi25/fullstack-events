9 SGPA

The "9 SGPA" event held on October 9, 2023, by the Full Stack Club provided students with actionable approaches to enhance grades, personality, and attendance. Attendees learned persuasive time management, syllabus understanding, and regular practice techniques. They also discovered the importance of consistent performance, active participation, and utilizing feedback to maintain excellent grades. Developing a strong personality through critical thinking, leadership, and teamwork was emphasized, alongside ensuring punctuality and attendance tracking.
